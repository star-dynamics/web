The Twelve
Archetypes

Every individual combines a primary and secondary mindset into one of twelve recognisable professional identities. Each archetype has its own motivational signature, workplace style, operating mode, and characteristic blind spot.

"The archetypes do not simplify people. They map them — turning psychological insight into actionable intelligence."
Socialiser-led Archetypes
S / T  ·  Socialiser-led
Thoughtful Advisor
Resonant Wisdom
Combines genuine care for people with the analytical depth to give advice that is both compassionate and sound. Maintains group harmony while ensuring output meets rigorous standards. The person you go to when you need someone who will listen properly and think clearly.
Aligner Mode
S / A  ·  Socialiser-led
Expressive Energiser
Infectious Momentum
Creates forward momentum through social energy and enthusiasm for new shared experiences. Uses charismatic energy to lift team spirit, especially during periods of change. The person who lifts a flat room and makes people excited about what the team could achieve together.
Driver Mode
S / R  ·  Socialiser-led
Grounded Connector
Reliable Warmth
Builds long-term, dependable relationships through consistency and loyalty. Anchors team trust through emotional consistency, remembering everyone's story and quietly holding the social fabric together. The person people turn to not for strategy, but for steadiness.
Aligner Mode
Thinker-led Archetypes
T / S  ·  Thinker-led
Structured Collaborator
Principled Cohesion
Uses analytical precision to build fair, transparent ways of working together. Applies methodical rigour to group dynamics, designing the meeting structures and decision-making processes that stop teams from falling apart. Believes the most effective teams are those managed with the same rigour as technical objectives.
Aligner Mode
T / A  ·  Thinker-led
Strategic Explorer
Structured Discovery
Attracted to bold ideas but tests every one with rigorous logic before committing. Innovates through the deconstruction of complex systems, combining visionary range with execution rigour. The person who can see the revolutionary possibility and simultaneously identify the three reasons it will fail, then propose a version that works.
Translator Mode
T / R  ·  Thinker-led
Precise Analyst
Unshakeable Clarity
Focused entirely on accuracy and evidence. Will say "that doesn't meet the standard" when everyone else is ready to move on — not to be difficult, but because letting something substandard through is genuinely unacceptable. Safeguards process integrity through vigilant adherence to standards.
Custodian Mode
Adventurer-led Archetypes
A / S  ·  Adventurer-led
Energetic Catalyst
Magnetic Possibility
Pushes boundaries and challenges conventions, using social charisma to bring people along for the ride. Arrives with an idea that makes everyone slightly uncomfortable, then makes that discomfort feel like excitement. Drives radical change by inspiring and mobilising the collective.
Driver Mode
A / T  ·  Adventurer-led
Inventive Pathfinder
Visionary Propulsion
Finds new paths by systematically taking apart existing assumptions. Explores uncharted territory through technical mastery and rapid experimentation, using a rigorous analytical filter to determine what is worth keeping and what should be discarded. Doesn't just say "do it differently" — explains precisely why the current approach is flawed.
Driver Mode
A / R  ·  Adventurer-led
Practical Builder
Constructive Agility
Turns ambitious ideas into tangible results through disciplined execution. Pairs the drive for the horizon with a requirement for methodical precision, turning ambitious dreams into undeniable reality. Identifies the goal with boldness, then delivers it with precision. The person who actually gets things built.
Translator Mode
Realist-led Archetypes
R / S  ·  Realist-led
Steady Harmoniser
Peaceful Continuity
Provides operational consistency through warmth and loyalty. Maintains long-term stability through group alignment, noticing when someone is struggling before anyone else does. Believes a stable system is one where everyone feels included and the social fabric is as strong as the operational process.
Custodian Mode
R / T  ·  Realist-led
Careful Guardian
Absolute Reliability
Ensures every process is followed and every standard is met, and genuinely understands why each one matters. Protects system integrity through rigorous analysis and duty, identifying and mitigating risks before they become systemic failures. The person who remembers why the rule exists and won't let you cut corners.
Custodian Mode
R / A  ·  Realist-led
Resilient Enabler
Adaptive Endurance
Creates stable platforms that allow others to take risks safely. Steady under normal conditions, fast to adapt when things go wrong. Builds the foundations upon which others can explore, ensuring that innovation never compromises structural integrity. The person you want in an emergency.
