What is STAR?

Not another personality tool.
A human operating system.

The STAR Framework challenges conventional approaches to understanding people. Rather than sorting individuals into fixed types or colour-coded boxes, it provides a dynamic, context-sensitive system for decoding why people behave the way they do, particularly under conditions of pressure and uncertainty.

Built on seven validated psychological theories, STAR synthesises decades of academic research into a single, coherent architecture. It moves beyond labelling to the stewardship of human potential, giving leaders, communicators, educators, and designers the tools to understand not just what people do, but why they do it.

The tools that dominated the last three decades — DISC, MBTI, Insights Discovery — were built for a different era. Their time is passing. STAR was built for the world as it is today, and the one that is fast arriving.
